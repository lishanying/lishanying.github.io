She has a good memory and she can remember all drawings she has seen.Today Alice designs a game using these drawings in her memory. Each grid has an integer on it(from 0 to K) representing the color on the corresponding position in the original drawing. Bob has to redraw the drawing with Alice’s information. Unfortunately, somtimes, the information Alice offers is wrong because of Alice’s poor math. And sometimes, Bob can work out multiple different drawings using the information Alice provides. Bob gets confused and he needs your help.
